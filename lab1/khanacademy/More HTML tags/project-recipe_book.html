Gerkules Kasha

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: with bread
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1.5 glasses
Milk 0.5 glass
Any kasha 5 spoons
Salt 1/4 of spoon
butter 5-10 g
sugar 5-10 g

Step 1: Prepare coarse oat flakes, high-quality water, salt, sugar and butter.
Step 2: Pour filtered or bottled water into a saucepan with a thick bottom.
Step 3: When the water boils, add salt, sugar and oat flakes. There should be more salt if the porridge is planned to be salty, and vice versa, if the porridge is sweet, then put more sugar.
Step 4: Bring the porridge to a boil, stir and boil on the lowest heat for about 15 - 30 minutes.

Eggs on frying pan

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serves: with bread and butter
Ingredients Quantity
Refined sunflower oil 1-2 spoons
eggs 3
sault 5-10 g

Step 1: Heat the frying pan over low heat.
Step 2: In the middle, carefully break the egg so that the yolk remains intact.
Step 3: Fry on low heat for about 7 minutes. The protein should seize, and the yolk inside should remain liquid. Add a little salt to the protein. At this stage, the fried eggs are ready.
